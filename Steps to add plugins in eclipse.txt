1. Go to Eclipse. 
2. Help
3. Eclipse marketplace -> A pop up will appear. 
4. Search for the required plugin. 
5. click on install. 
6. Select or unselect the feature. 
7. Click on Confirm. 
8. Select 'I accept the terms of the license agreements'
9. Finish. 
10. Eclipse will start installing the plugin.
11. Eclipse will display a Warning pop-up, click OK. 
12. Once the features are installed Eclipse will ask you to restart the Eclipse for the changes to take effect. Click Yes.
13. Once the Eclipse has restarted you can verify whether the plugin was properly installed or not. Go to Help=>Eclipse Marketplace… then click on the ‘Installed‘ tab. Here you can see the list of plugins that are been installed.
